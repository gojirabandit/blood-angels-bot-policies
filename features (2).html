The Sanguinius Bot serves the Sons of Baal, bringing the wisdom and authority of the Great Angel to the warriors of the Angels Imperialis Blood Angels. Below is a complete list of features and commands available to all brothers of the Chapter.

I

📋Character Cards

Create and manage your warrior's profile in the Chapter archives.

/create_character_card

Establish your warrior's character card in the Chapter archives. Set your Roblox username, character name, timezone, and optional lore details including a character quote, history, and image.

/view_character_card

View character cards for yourself or another warrior. Displays all statistics, operations, qualifications, commendations, active oaths, penances, and current status.

/edit_character_card

Update your character card details via a form. Edit your Roblox username, character name, timezone, quote, or history — supports up to ~1,000 words of lore.

/edit_character_image

Update the image displayed on your character card by providing a direct image URL.

/archive_character_card

Archive your current character card and start fresh. All data is safely preserved and can be viewed or restored at any time.

/view_archived_cards

View your personal collection of archived character cards, showing the 3 most recent.

/unarchive_character_card

Restore a previously archived character card back to active status. You must not have an active card to restore one.

/delete_character_card

Permanently remove your character card from the Chapter archives. This action cannot be undone — consider archiving instead.

II

⚔️Operations

Submit and track participation in Chapter operations and battles against Chaos.

/log_operation

Submit an operation for approval. Includes operation name, difficulty, enemy type (Chaos), mission type, a required screenshot, and optional notes. Operations are reviewed before being recorded to your card.

/edit_operation

Modify a previously approved operation on your card. Update name, difficulty, enemy type, mission type, notes, or screenshot URL. All edits are logged.

/character_card_leaderboard

View the top 10 warriors ranked by various categories including Total Points, Total Missions, FPD, OPD, Oaths Fulfilled, or Chaos Operations.

V

🏅Commendations

/commend_warrior

Award a commendation to a fellow warrior for exceptional service. Commendations are tracked on their character card. A 24-hour cooldown applies per recipient.

🩸 You cannot commend yourself. Warriors must have a character card to receive commendations.

VI

📜Oaths of the Hunt

Swear sacred oaths and track their fulfillment.

/oath_of_the_hunt

Swear a personal Oath of the Hunt before your battle-brothers. Your oath will be displayed on your character card until fulfilled or cancelled. Only one active oath is permitted at a time.

/oath_fulfilledAuthorized

Mark a warrior's active oath as fulfilled. Increments their oath fulfillment counter on their character card and permanently records the achievement.

🩸 Oaths can be cancelled via the Cancel button on your character card. All oath activity is logged.

VII

⛓️Penances

Assign penance for infractions against the Chapter.

/assign_penanceAuthorized

Assign penance to a warrior with flexible duration (specify days and/or hours). While under penance, the warrior's character card displays in black.

/remove_penanceAuthorized

Lift an active penance from a warrior before its natural expiration. A reason for early removal is required and logged.

🩸 All penance assignments and removals are logged to the Penance Log channel.
